White Sauce Pasta!

White sauce pasta is a classic and easy student dish. Takes you about 5 minutes. You need

  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Garlic
  • Black Pepper
  • Herbs (Parsley, Oregano, Dried Basil)
  • Pasta

Melt the butter and cook the garlic until fragrant. Add in the flour into the butter FIRST. Afterwards stir in milk till desired consistency. Pepper, herb and salt to taste. Add the cooked pasta and enjoy!
